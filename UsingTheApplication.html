Using the Application

The comparator application shows the performance measure data for individual routes; each route is delineated by direction. Follow these steps to access and display information in the application:

  1. Click the Route button to select a route.
  2. Click the Performance Measure button to select a performance measure.
  3. Click the Direction button to select the route direction.
  4. Click the Peak Time Period button to select the peak period (AM or PM).
  5. Click the Year button to select data from the year 2012 or 2015, or to compare the 2012 and 2015 data.

Displays

  1. Click the Map display to view the performance data for the selected route in a detailed map.
  2. Click the Chart display to view both a bar chart and line graph representing the selected route and performance data.
  3. Click the Table display to view a table of attributes and performance data for each roadway segment on the selected route.

Data Sources/CMP Standards

The data displayed by the Express Highway Performance Comparator comes from the following sources:

  • INRIX
  • MassDOT’s Road Inventory
  • Central Transportation Planning Staff (CTPS) of the Boston Region Metropolitan Planning Organization (MPO)
  • Massachusetts Department of Transportation (MassDOT)
  • Massachusetts Office of Geographic Information (MassGIS)

INRIX provided the speed data in one-minute increments per TMC, as a sample of 63 non-holiday Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays in spring and fall 2015. CTPS collected and processed the data, and calculated the performance measures. The CMP defines peak periods for limited-access roadways as follows: AM peak period—6:00 AM to 10:00 AM; PM peak period—3:00 PM to 7:00 PM. Free-flow speeds and travel times were collected on the same days between 12:00 AM and 4:00 AM.
